The Challenge

IndiGo dominates the domestic market but faces saturation. The goal was to identify profitable international routes while navigating complex constraints like Bilateral Rights, Fleet Range, and Competitor Capacity (Emirates/Tata Group).

Tech Stack

Power BI Advanced Excel DAX Financial Modeling

Strategic Verdict

The analysis proves a "Regional Dominance" strategy (Middle East/SE Asia) yields 35% higher margins than a "Global Long-Haul" strategy due to current fleet wet-lease costs.

1. Executive Profitability Overview

Power BI / Heatmap Analysis

Insight: The Profitability Heatmap identifies BOM-DXB and DEL-DXB as high-volume anchors, while highlighting European routes (Red) as currently loss-making due to wet-lease costs.

2. Operational Efficiency (Breakeven)

Power BI / Clustered Column Chart

Insight: Breakeven Load Factor analysis reveals that Tier-2 routes like Trichy-Singapore (TRZ-SIN) have a massive safety margin (Actual LF > Breakeven LF), making them financially safer bets than high-profile European launches.

3. Market Capacity & Seasonality

Power BI / Donut & Line Charts

Insight: Delhi (DEL) controls 42% of capacity. Seasonality trends indicate a critical "Cash Conservation" period in July-September (Monsoon), suggesting maintenance scheduling during this trough to maximize Q3/Q4 availability.
